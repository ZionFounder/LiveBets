🎡 How to Play

  1. 🌹 Spin the Wheel - The host spins a digital wheel to randomly select the game.
  2. 🔢 Reserve Your Number (1–6) - Players reserve numbers by donating. Once all six are taken, the game starts.
  3. 🎮 Game is Played Live - Performed on stream. No edits. No retakes.
  4. 🏆 1st, 2nd, 3rd Win - Three numbers are picked by gameplay outcome. Prizes go out instantly.
