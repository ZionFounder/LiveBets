🧠 About Us

24/7 Live Games is a high-energy livestream betting experiment that blends entertainment, community, and decentralized tokenomics. We’re not a flashy studio-backed project—we’re building this from scratch, DIY-style, with raw passion and community-first vision.

From our humble beginnings using basic props and a single camera, we plan to scale into a full-scale, studio-grade game experience fueled by community support. This is your chance to be part of something real—built from the ground up.

🎡 How to Play

  1. 🌹 Spin the Wheel — We spin to pick the game for the round.
  2. 🔢 Choose Your Number (1–6) — Reserve by donating and submitting your wallet.
  3. 🎮 Game Is Live — All games are streamed in real-time with no pre-recording.
  4. 🏆 Win Big — 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place numbers win real crypto prizes.

🎮 Available Games

  • 🎲 Dice Roll — Unique rolls determine winners. Reroll on repeats.
  • 🚗 Card Draw — Drawn live until three winning numbers appear.
  • 🔸 Ball Drop (Plinko) — Balls drop into 3 different slots.
  • 🐸 Meme Race — Crypto mascots race. Winners determined in rounds.
  • 🪙 Coin Flip — 1v1 elimination rounds. Last 3 standing win.

↺ Games rotate weekly with community-selected additions.
