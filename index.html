🧠 About Us

24/7 Live Games is an unpredictable, high-energy livestream betting platform built for entertainment, community, and deflationary crypto economics. This isn’t just a game — it’s an experiment in building something real, raw, and rewarding from the ground up.

We’re not launching with studio lighting and million-dollar sets. We’re starting this in a room, with makeshift props, DIY games, and a big vision. Over time, with the community’s support, we’ll grow this into a full-blown livestreaming production — with custom game setups, studio-grade streaming, and a vibrant ecosystem that gives back.

Whether you’re watching, betting, or just vibing with the chat, you’re part of something we’re building together — from scratch.

🕹️ How to Play

  1. 🌹 Spin the Wheel - The host spins a digital wheel to randomly select the game.
  2. 🔢 Reserve Your Number (1–6) - Players reserve numbers by staking. Once all six numbers are taken, the game begins.
  3. 🎮 Game is Played Live - The selected game is performed live on stream — with no pre-recorded outcomes.
  4. 🏆 1st, 2nd, and 3rd Place Win - Three unique winning numbers are determined based on how the game unfolds. Winnings are paid out instantly.

🎮 Available Games

  • 🎲 Dice Roll - Roll one die. First three unique rolls are ranked. Rerolls if duplicates appear.
  • 🚗 Card Draw - Shuffled deck drawn live. First three matching 1–6 win positions.
  • 🟢 Ball Drop (Plinko Style) - Balls dropped until three unique slots are hit.
  • 🐸 Meme Race (Crypto Edition) - Meme characters race. First finishes = 1st place. Race resets for 2nd and 3rd.
  • 🪙 Coin Flip - 1v1 elimination. Last 3 standing take the top 3 prizes.

🔄 Games rotate weekly. New games are tested and added regularly based on feedback.
